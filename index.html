Robin McLaurin Williams (July 21, 1951 - August 11, 2014)

He was known for his improvisation skills and the wide variety of memorable characters. He is regarded as one of the greatest comedians of all time. He was in the movies:

  • Dead Poets Society
  • Jumanji
  • Mrs. Doubtfire
  • Good Will Hunting
  • Night Museum
  • Patch Adams

and a ton more.
